What is Giftscop?

Giftscop is a recreation that was started July of 2018.

The developers are James9270, ugng, Hector Rains, marvin, red, ChocolateJake, Elphont, Wirelex.exe, Noogai223, Caylie C, Re-Crix and venen.

The recreation is being made in Unity and is currently (03/21/19) on version 1.0 (Public Build)
